Garden design, landscaping, maintenance and planting services in Bath & Bristol.

Services

From an initial concept, right through the design and build process, to expert aftercare, we provide a complete in-house experience for all your garden project needs.

Design

Great gardens start with great design! We understand the importance of both form and function, so our gardens not only look the part, but they're also designed to fit your lifestyle.

From initial consultation through to the final plans, we'll always listen carefully to understand your requirements and our bespoke mood board curation and hand-drawn designs ensure that you are happy every step of the way.

Build

A bespoke build is a wonderful way to elevate your garden, we are passionate about creating spaces that are a joy for our clients to use - from the addition of a natural stone terrace to creating an area for entertaining, a shaded pergola drenched in sweet-smelling climbers, or a summer house offering a sanctuary for reading, writing or relaxing.

Our team have years of experience in all areas of garden construction, including general landscaping, stonemasonry and brick work, or specialised carpentry.

Plant

When done correctly, planting has the power to transform a garden. Our extensive knowledge, practical skills and high-quality resources mean we can source and implement stunning displays that bring your outdoor space to life!

From bountiful borders, cottage-style beds, heavenly rose gardens, Mediterranean-esque patios, herb planters, or naturally styled meadows, we strive to create an atmosphere that's right for you, with seasonal planting that works all year-round.

Maintenance

Whether you need help to look after your new garden or want a hand to renovate and improve your existing garden, our team of skilled gardeners are on hand to help.

We can offer bespoke maintenance plans and scheduled visits to ensure you get peace of mind that your garden is being cared for, covering all aspects of garden maintenance such as border maintenance, expert pruning, lawns and hedges any much more.

We can also advise on additional services such as irrigation, garden lighting, topiary and planter supply and implementation.

Get to know us

Jemma

The Team

Jemma Smallman   |   Director

Jemma has over 10 years' experience in horticulture, during which time she has worked on a number of beautiful and prestigious sites in and around Bath, including multiple gardens for the Duchy of Cornwall, listed city centre properties and large estate gardens.

After training as a skilled gardener and designer in her previous role, working alongside another highly decorated garden designer, Jemma wanted to expand on her passion for the build side of projects and went on to form Viridis Landscapes in 2020.

Jemma loves being on site come rain or shine but also has a huge passion for the design process behind the scenes, and her passion for creativity and functionality has enabled her to develop and craft her own original style that balances practicality and beauty.

How much does a new garden cost?

At the start of our design process, we will always ask if you have a budget in mind for the work, and more often than not, it's not something a lot of clients know how to answer.

Every garden project is unique, and the costs will vary depending on numerous factors such as access, level of skilled work involved, specific material costs etc. Because of this we will always provide an initial consultation and quote that's bespoke to your requirements and discuss what is likely to be involved in terms of costs before committing to a full design.

FAQ

What size projects do you take on?

We work on all sizes of garden projects - everything from Balcony planters, to small city centre courtyards, new build family gardens up to large country estates.

How much is a garden design?

We offer a range of garden design services, depending on your needs. Once we have established the size of your garden, and obtained some basic information from your regarding your requirements and desires, we can give you a better idea on costs.

Our planting plans start from £495 + vat and full garden design packages start from £895 + vat

We also offer a one hour garden consultation, in which we will arrange a visit to the site with you to look at the garden together and make suggestions and recommendations for what could be done. This is followed up by a written report and is charged at £150.

How many weeks?

This is dependent on the complexity and size of the project.

Most garden designs can take between 6-8 weeks from initial point of acceptance of quote, with a medium sized build project potentially taking around 4-6 weeks.

We recommend contacting us with details of your project to get a more accurate idea on timescales, and suggest to plan ahead as we are usually booked up 3-4 months in advance.
