High-quality garden design, landscaping, maintenance and planting services.

Services

Skillful design, reliable building, and show-stopping planting. Our range of garden services in Bath and the surrounding area is varied and always exceptionally delivered.

Design

Great gardens start with great design! We understand the importance of both form and function, so our gardens not only look the part, they're also designed to fit your lifestyle. From initial consultation through to the final plans, we'll always listen carefully to understand your requirements and our bespoke mood board curation and hand-drawn designs ensure that you are happy every step of the way.

Build

A bespoke build is a wonderful way to elevate your garden, from the addition of an arbour offering a romantic place to sit, a shaded pergola drenched in sweet-smelling climbers, or a summer house offering a sanctuary for reading, writing or relaxing. Our team have years of experience in all areas of garden construction, be it general landscaping, stonemasonry and brick work, or specialised carpentry.

Plant

When done correctly, planting has the power to transform a garden. Our extensive knowledge, practical skills and high-quality resources mean we can source and implement stunning displays that bring your outdoor space to life! From bountiful borders, cottage-style beds, heavenly rose gardens, Mediterranean-esque patios, herb planters, or naturally-styled meadow areas, we strive to create an atmosphere that's right for you, with seasonal planting that works all year-round.

Get to know us

Jemma

The Team

Jemma has 10+ years in garden design and landscaping, during which time she has worked on a number of beautiful and prestigious sites including multiple gardens for the Duchy of Cornwall. Jemma loves being on site come rain or shine but also has a huge passion for the design process behind the scenes, and her passion for creativity and functionality has enabled her to develop and craft her own original style that balances practicality and beauty.
